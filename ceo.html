The Policy Implementation and Alignment Branch coordinates the development, implementation, and alignment of an extensive range of countywide policies and initiatives focused on the Board of Supervisors’ highest priorities to create a most effective, just, and equitable LA County. These focus areas include initiatives that are:

  • Mission critical to the County
  • Large-scale and complex
  • Involve collaboration across multiple departments
  • In need of project management to be successful
  • Time-limited or can be transitioned to a department for implementation
  • In between the scope of departments that they cannot solve on their own
