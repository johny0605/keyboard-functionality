Board of Supervisors

The Board of Supervisors is Los Angeles County’s governing body, serving as the executive and legislative head of the largest and most complex county government in the nation. The five elected members of the Board of Supervisors from left to right are Janice Hahn, Hilda L. Solis, Lindsey P. Horvath, Kathryn Barger and Holly J. Mitchell.
