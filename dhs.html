For a list of resources to help those impacted by the wildfires CLICK HERE.

For our patients who lost medications due to fires/evacuations, please call your pharmacy for a refill or your clinic for a renewal. Additional support for lost medications available at Nurse Advice Line 7 a.m. -7 p.m. 7 days a week at 844-804-0055.

LA Health Nurse Advice Line

Care and support is just a call away. 7am-7pm, 7 days a week. Call us Toll Free 844-804-0055, Option 2.

eVisits for Online Care

With eVisits you can access care 7 days a week through the LA Health Portal with no appointment needed. Get started by logging on to the LA Health Portal and filling out an eVisit form.
